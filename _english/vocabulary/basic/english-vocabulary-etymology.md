subrogate = sub- + reg-(rog) + ate
sub-: in the place of, under
reg-: move in a straight line
(人员)取代；【法】取代
to substitute one person for another,
especially in transferring a right or claim

surrogate
替代的；代用的
used to describe a person or thing
that takes the place of, or is used instead of, sb/sth else
